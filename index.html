Save Spot - Electricity Consumption Tracking App

Save Spot is a user-friendly tool designed to help users monitor and track their energy usage. The app provides real-time data on electricity consumption, helping you identify areas where you can reduce energy usage and save money on electricity bills.

How It Works

Track and monitor energy usage with NB IoT device, obtain reports with visualized data and intelligent suggestions to reduce consumption.

1

Track Consumption Trends

Monitor daily, weekly and monthly energy usage trends with our NB IOT device plugged into your household's main board.

2

Generate Usage Report

Based on the data collected, our app generates a report with visualized usage trend comparisons Vs target, for a given period

3

Get Intelligent Suggestions

Our app provides intelligent suggestions to reduce high consumption if usage is predicted to surpass monthly targets based on predicted/forecasted usage

Features

Our feature-packed app helps you save energy and money with real-time power cut updates, ML based bill forecasting, and more valuable insights and tools for achieving your energy goals.

Usage Tracking

Via the IoT device, the app captures and compares daily consumption along with your MTD running electricity bill

Setup Goals

Set ambitious goals for your unit consumption or bill amount and receive timely alerts to keep you on track. Connect with the community and share your progress.

Benchmarking

Benchmark consumption against competitors and forecast future bills using Machine Learning based on SME category with our app.

Smart Tips

Access energy-saving tips tailored to your routines, including appliance-related advice and tips for modifying behaviors, to help you save energy and reduce your carbon footprint.

Community

Share your successes and insights with the community and on social media platforms.

News

Stay up-to-date with energy-related news and get real-time updates on power cuts in your area with our app's news feed and dynamic schedule tailored to your location.

ML Models

Our mobile application utilizes advanced regression models to calculate the month-to-date bill, taking into account various factors such as tax amendments and usage-based unit rates. Additionally, we employ LSTM machine learning models to accurately forecast future electricity bills. SME benchmarking is also available, providing users with an overview of their current usage stage compared to similar businesses.
