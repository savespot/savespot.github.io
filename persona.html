Nadeesha Silva

CEO of a tech company

40 years old and lives in Colombo, Sri Lanka

Nadeesha is a busy executive who values her time and is committed to running an environmentally sustainable household. She recently invested in a high-end smart home automation system and is interested in using technology to optimize her energy consumption and save money on her electricity bills. She is interested in using a smart energy meter and associated app to monitor her energy usage in real-time and identify areas where she can reduce waste and improve efficiency.

Motivations

  • Is interested in using technology to optimize her energy consumption and reduce her electricity bills.
  • Prefers solutions that are easy to use and integrate with her existing smart home automation system.
  • Is willing to invest in high-quality energy-efficient appliances and solutions if they will provide a positive return on investment.
  • Is interested in integrating her energy management system with her home security and climate control systems.

Frustrations

  • High energy costs impact her household budget and make it difficult to remain competitive in the luxury travel market.
  • Concerns about the environmental impact of her household and the potential impact on her brand image.
  • Limited resources for managing her energy consumption and implementing new technology solutions.
  • Limited availability of high-quality energy-efficient appliances and solutions in Sri Lanka.

Needs

  • Wants to reduce her environmental impact and operate an eco-friendly household.
  • Wants to track her energy consumption in real-time to identify areas where she can reduce waste and improve efficiency.
  • Wants to receive personalized recommendations on how to reduce her energy consumption based on her specific usage patterns and needs.
