This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use "Save Spot" (the "App"). By using our App, you agree to the terms and conditions outlined in this Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2023/03/10

1. Collection of Information

We may collect the following types of personal information from you:

  • Contact information (such as name and email address) when you register for the App
  • Electricity consumption data that you provide to the App or that is collected by the smart energy meter
  • Location information (such as your address) if you choose to allow the App to access your device's location to provide the power cut schedule
  • Information related to your use of the App, such as your device type, operating system, and App usage statistics

2. Use of Information

We may use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To provide you with access to and use of the App
  • To collect and analyze electricity consumption data for the purposes of providing you with personalized recommendations on reducing energy consumption and saving money on your electricity bills
  • To share the Consumption Trend Data with third parties based
  • To respond to your inquiries and requests
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Sharing of Information

We may share your personal information in the following ways:

  • With third-party service providers who assist us in providing the App, such as hosting providers and analytics services
  • With affiliates and partners who help us provide the App or who offer complementary products or services
  • With law enforcement or government officials in response to a subpoena, court order, or other legal process, or to comply with applicable laws and regulations

4. Security of Information

We take appropriate measures to safeguard your personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your personal information.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

6. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, and will post the updated Privacy Policy on our website. Your continued use of the App after any changes to the Privacy Policy indicates your agreement to the updated Privacy Policy.

If you have any questions or concerns about these terms and conditions, please contact us at csupport@savespot.com
